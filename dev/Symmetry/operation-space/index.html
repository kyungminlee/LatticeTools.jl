Symmetry Operations

AbstractSpaceSymmetryOperation{S} (where S is the type for the coordinates) represent spatial symmetry, including translation, and point operation, and the combination of the two.

Translation Operation

The type TranslationOperation{S} represents translation operation on the space of coordinates of type S.

A translation operation can be of integer type.

TranslationOperation([1, 2])
TranslationOperation{Int64}([1, 2])

It can also represent translation of non-integer type

TranslationOperation([0.5, 0.0, 0.5])
TranslationOperation{Float64}([0.5, 0.0, 0.5])
TranslationOperation([1//2, 3//4])
TranslationOperation{Rational{Int64}}(Rational{Int64}[1//2, 3//4])

A translation operation can be applied to a coordinate

t = TranslationOperation([1, 2])
@show t([3, 4])
@show apply_operation(t, [3, 4])
2-element Array{Int64,1}:
 4
 6

Point Operation

p = PointOperation([0 1; 1 0])
@show p([3, 4])
2-element Array{Int64,1}:
 4
 3
